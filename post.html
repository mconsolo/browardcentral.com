Marina Oaks Condos, Fort Lauderdale, FL

Find similar condos for sale to the Marina Oaks Condos, Fort Lauderdale, FL

Marina Oaks is in the south western part of Fort Lauderdale. It is a safe and central location. The condominium comes with two tennis courts and a outdoor pool. If you want to live in a nice area that is not overly expensive, yet close to the beach then you can find your home in Marina Oaks. 


What kind of units does Marina Oaks offer? 

All units in the complex offer a modern appearance with either a 1/1 or 2/2 bedroom layout. Square footage ranges from 920 to 1100, depending on the unit. All bedrooms come with a walk in closet so you have plenty of space for your wardrobe. All kitchens come with up stainless steel appliances and updated cabinets. Each unit also includes a screened in porch. Parking is plentiful with plenty of extra spaces for everyone in the complex plus more for guests. Price range to buy a unit in this complex are from $72,000 to $130,000. Rent for the units range from $900 to $1,300. 

What are the buildings like?

Condo buildings are non-waterfront properties built from 1988 to 2006. There is a north side of complexes and a south side of complexes. The south side has the outdoor pool and tennis courts which are easy walking distance for any owner. HOA dues are $265 a month. Dues include yard maintenance, trash removal and upkeep of the tennis and pool areas. Common areas include the pool and tennis courts that are open year round.

I like the Condo but what about the surrounding area?

As mentioned before these condos are in the south western section of Fort Lauderdale. Nearby you have easy access to I95 and I595. Having easy access to the highways makes it easier to travel to surrounding cities like Miami, Boca Raton and West Palm Beach. Fort Lauderdale Marina is less than a mile away if you would need to keep you boat somewhere safe and protected. There is also great restaurants close by like Rendezvous Waterfront Bar & Grill and Lil Red's BBQ spot. Fort Lauderdale offers a lot of job opportunities downtown which is only a 5-10 minute drive. Other places that are close to work are in Plantation, Davie and Sunrise, all 15 minutes or less. Going to the beach is also very close, only a 8 minute drive to Fort Lauderdale beach and 15 minute drive to Hallandale Beach.

The Marina Oaks Condos in Fort Lauderdale are a great place to call home. You will not be disappointed if you end up living here. You can find condos for sale in Marina Oaks (if there are any available today) as well as all available Fort Lauderdale condos for sale on my website.

Homes for Sale in the River Oaks Neighborhood Fort Lauderdale, FL


Why Do People Live here?
River Oaks is a beautiful neighborhood of single family homes that is conveniently located just 1.5 miles to Downtown Fort Lauderdale and 3.5 miles to the beach. This neighborhood has a great range of people in the neighborhood. From young professionals to families with young children to retirees. River Oaks is a very central location with quick access to highways and convenient shopping locations. Interstate 595 gives quick access to the west all the way out to Sunrise and I95 takes you south to Miami and North to West Palm and Boca. It's far enough from the main hustle and bustle of the city that residents don't feel like the urban center of Fort Lauderdale is knocking on their door yet close enough to everything you need.

Looking for a home in River Oaks?

What's the Neighborhood Like?

The homes you would expect to find for sale in the River Oaks Neighborhood are generally going to range between 1,200 and 3,000 square feet. Most of the homes were originally built in the 1950's and some are still being built today. Since then, many of the homes have had updates and the neighborhood is kept in very nice condition. The community of River Oaks cares about the area and citizens do a great job in keep it nice. The smallest homes in the neighborhood start with 2 bedrooms and 1 bath while the larger floor plans have a six bedroom, 4 bath layout. Most of the lots range between 1/4 acre and a 1/2 acre so there is plenty of room for people to get out and enjoy the beautiful South Florida weather.

What's Going on Nearby the River Oaks Neighborhood?

River Oaks is located in the south western part of Fort Lauderdale. Fort Lauderdale Marina is on the south western part of the subdivision. If you have a boat and would like it professionally taken care of the Marina is not far away. River Oaks has just about every kind of conceivable shopping in walking distance. The Southland shopping center is just down the street and has everything from a pharmacy to a local coffee shop. Broward County is even close by, within a 5 minute drive. In the evenings you will want to take a stroll down to enjoy dinner at Lil Red's BBQ, a great neighborhood restaurant. The beach is just miles away, so any day you feel like getting some sun and enjoying the ocean it is a short drive.
